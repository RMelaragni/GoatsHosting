Account sharing and concurrent steams

Can I share my account?
No. However, you may have the account on multiple of your own devices.
How many concurrent steams can I have?
3 from the same IP address. This generally means anyone in your home on your network can watch.
1 from a mobile device off your home network. Example: When streaming from a cell phone while out of your home.

Can you tell if I share my acount?
Easily. We don't have a person actively monitoring you. However, we do have robust security software.
